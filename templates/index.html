Welcome

This is the Nesta Added Lipids Levy tool. It will look at a food recipe and use AI to determine if any unnecessary lipids have been added, and calculate a levy of £1 for every extra 250 calores this adds. The intention is that this levy is applied for food being produced for consumption by the general public where the levy is charged.

Click the Recipe button to get started.

How it works

Unnecessary lipids here are any compounds containing fats, that have been added to enhance flavour as opposed to being a key ingredient. For many food items, these are needed to an extent as they impact the texture and consistency of the product, but to a point. Going beyond this point will risk incurring a levy.

For example: A run-of-the-mill cheeseburger with a single burger patty, cheese slice and bun would not incur a levy, but if you add 4 extra cheese slices (at approximately 100 cals each) and then butter the buns with 1 tablespoon of butter (approximately 100 cals), then you've added an extra 500 calories and so a £2 levy would be added.
